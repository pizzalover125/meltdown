What is Meltdown?

Meltdown is Phoenix's premier 12-hour hackathon designed to ignite innovation and creativity in the desert heat. This isn't just another coding competition – it's a transformative experience where students push boundaries, forge friendships, and bring their wildest tech ideas to life.

Whether you're a seasoned developer or have never written a line of code, Meltdown provides the perfect environment to learn, create, and showcase your skills. Join us for an unforgettable journey of discovery, collaboration, and innovation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate? +
Anyone under 18 with a passion for technology and innovation! Whether you're a complete beginner or a coding expert, there's a place for you at Meltdown.
What should I bring? +
Bring your laptop, charger, a water bottle, and an open mind. We'll provide food, drinks, WiFi, and a comfortable workspace.
Do I need a team? +
Not at all! You can work solo or we'll help you find an amazing team during our team formation session at the beginning of the event.
What can I build? +
Anything you can imagine! Websites, mobile apps, games, hardware projects, AI tools – the sky's the limit. We'll provide theme prompts to inspire you.
Are there prizes? +
Yes! We'll have prizes for various categories including Most Creative, Best Beginner Project, and People's Choice. But the real prize is the experience!
Will there be mentors? +
Absolutely! We'll have experienced mentors available throughout the event to help with technical challenges, brainstorming, and project guidance.
What about food? +
We've got you covered! Meals, snacks, and drinks will be provided throughout the 12 hours. We'll accommodate dietary restrictions too.
